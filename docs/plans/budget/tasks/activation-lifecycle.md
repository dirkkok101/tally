## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- A current/future Draft with trusted authority, actor, reason, key, compatible category version, and currently active category IDs becomes Active.
- If another revision is Active, one BEGIN IMMEDIATE atomically supersedes it, activates the Draft, updates the plan pointer, appends ordered attributable events, and stores replay references.
- Success yields exactly one Active; exact replay returns the original activation and supersession event snapshots even after later lifecycle changes.
- Closed period, missing authority/attribution, non-Draft/wrong-plan revision, stale contract, or unknown/inactive/archived category fails before any lifecycle change.
- Concurrency, storage, cancellation, interruption, or resource failure leaves either the complete prior or complete new state, never partial/multiply-active.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT auto-activate on draft, reopen Closed periods, reactivate Superseded revisions, change immutable content, or infer authority.
- Do NOT validate categories before loading the exact Draft and trusted period, or bypass the public Ledger client and mutation executor.
