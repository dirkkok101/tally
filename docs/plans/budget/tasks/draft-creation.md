## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- A complete current/future request validates actor, reason, key, unique stable category IDs, active lifecycle, category contract version, and exact nonnegative Int64 minor-unit amounts before mutation.
- Success creates or reuses one period plan, a new immutable sequential Draft revision, exact explicit entries including zero, and one attributable DraftCreated event; active_revision_id is unchanged.
- Empty, all-zero, omitted/unbudgeted, and nonempty drafts remain distinct; checked totals round-trip exactly.
- Closed periods, negative/malformed/overflow amounts, duplicate/display-name-only/unknown/inactive/archived/stale categories, or missing attribution fail with no plan or revision change.
- Revising any Draft/Active/Superseded content creates a new Draft and never modifies or copies hidden intent from the source.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT activate on save, copy omitted categories, persist names or period state, mutate earlier content, accept archived categories, or perform partial entry writes.
- Do NOT query private Ledger storage or bypass BudgetMutationExecutor.
