## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- Inject failures before/after validation, replay lookup, revision insert, entry insert, prior supersession, activation, active pointer, events, outcome references, commit, and result delivery.
- After each restart, observe either zero mutation or exactly one complete revision/lifecycle/outcome chain; no partial revision, missing event, duplicate Draft, stale pointer, or multiple Active rows.
- Retry every interrupted key and prove pre-commit retries commit once while post-commit retries return the exact original event-time result.
- History sequences, prior/result statuses, replacement IDs, actor/reason, hashes, and active pointer reconcile after recovery.
- WAL/SHM/temp cleanup and permission state remain owner-only after forced termination.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT simulate transactions with mocks or accept in-process assertions without reopening a real database.
- Do NOT repair partial rows in tests, weaken FULL sync, skip result-delivery cutpoints, or compare only aggregate row counts.
