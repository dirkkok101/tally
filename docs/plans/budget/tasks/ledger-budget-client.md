## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- ListBudgetCategoriesAsync and GetBudgetCategoryAsync preserve stable IDs, lifecycle, descriptor compatibility, errors, and cancellation.
- QueryBudgetActualsAsync maps the half-open period to inclusive dates, consumes every page and ordinal once, checks shared snapshot/generation evidence, and returns exact signed values.
- Incompatibility, unavailable operations, bad cursors, missing ordinals, unknown categories, and expected Ledger failures yield no partial position.
- The concrete client stays additive and never spawns a process or catches unexpected exceptions into payloads.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT add a client interface, transport abstraction, HTTP, process, SQL, or direct handler seam.
- Do NOT duplicate Ledger calculations, drop CancellationToken, rewrite errors, retain actual payloads, or break INGEST/CLASSIFY methods.
