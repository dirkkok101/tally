## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- ledger.category.list/get expose stable category ID, current display name, Active or Archived lifecycle, and compatibility evidence in deterministic order.
- ledger.actuals.query accepts inclusive dates and returns one complete snapshot with identity, ordinal, nullable category ID, lifecycle evidence, signed budgetActualMinor, generation, and exact full-set total.
- Ledger applies transaction lifecycle, current allocation, owned-transfer exclusion, refund netting, and categorization semantics; BUDGET consumes them verbatim.
- All pages share one snapshot, contain every ordinal exactly once, reconcile totals, and fail atomically on expiry, cursor, version, lifecycle, or integrity errors.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT create a private BUDGET Ledger query, open LedgerDb, expose storage rows, or duplicate Ledger finance semantics.
- Do NOT pass an exclusive end boundary as Ledger effectiveDateTo.
- Do NOT drop archived assigned IDs, treat them as uncategorized, or mutate Ledger facts.

### Expected Outputs

- LedgerBudgetCompositionContracts
- Budget Actual and category lifecycle projection behavior
- Focused public-contract tests
