## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- Create 0700 BUDGET directories and 0600 database, WAL, SHM, lock, and recognized temporary files; reject unsafe ownership or modes.
- Apply ordered user_version migrations with foreign_keys, WAL, FULL sync, bounded busy handling, and all five designed tables/constraints.
- Enforce unique ZAR month plans, immutable revision content/entries/provenance/hashes/events, Draft to Active to Superseded, and at most one Active.
- One BEGIN IMMEDIATE spans replay lookup, conflict, revision/event changes, active pointer, prior supersession, and outcome references.
- Never persist actuals, category authority, snapshots, cursors, positions, reports, consumer state, or serialized request/response bodies.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT share ledger.db, use flat JSON, EF/DbContext, hosted storage, generic repositories, provider abstractions, or custom crypto.
- Do NOT update/delete immutable history, cascade-delete references, persist caller period state, raw keys, serialized payloads, actuals, cursors, or category names.
