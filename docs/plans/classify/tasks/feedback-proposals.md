## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- Accepted, rejected, or corrected feedback appends exact outcome, transaction, evaluation, normalization, rule set, contributing rule versions, owner, reason, and timestamp.
- Completed correction feedback stores authoritative prior and resulting Ledger allocation IDs without rewriting either allocation.
- The deterministic builder records no proposal or one smallest-scope retire, narrow, or replacement draft and never broadens or activates it.
- Unavailable prior MatchEvidence stores the owner decision but creates no proposal reconstructed from current state.
- Historical feedback remains linked to original outcomes and rules after retirement or supersession.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT infer a general rule from one correction, auto-activate a proposal, or change broad authority.
- Do NOT rewrite Ledger allocation history or prior CLASSIFY feedback.
- Do NOT reconstruct missing evidence from current transaction, rule, or category state.
- Do NOT expose private descriptions or normalized tokens in results or diagnostics.

### Expected Outputs

- RecordClassificationFeedbackCommand
- FeedbackProposalBuilder
- ClassificationFeedbackStore

### Constraints

- Feedback and proposals are append-only; any later activation uses the normal validation and activation path.

### Notes

- A proposal is non-active typed data, not an executable rule change.
