## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- A private owner-only post-INGEST sample spans at least 90 days, every row has an expected outcome, and the report contains fingerprints and aggregates only.
- Evidence shows at least 20 recurring category decisions per month and the measured manual Ledger workflow exceeds 15 minutes per month; otherwise the gate fails and routes to the do-less workflow.
- Held-out evaluation shows at least 50 percent fewer per-transaction owner decisions with zero incorrect batch-applied assignments; otherwise the gate fails and custom classifier work stops.
- The exact v1 vocabulary accounts for every row, preserves drift canaries, emits no unexplained incompatible-category conflict, and requires no cloud, remote embedding, daemon, or data export.
- The tracked report records only counts, fingerprints, threshold outcomes, environment identity, and timestamp; private paths and payload values are absent.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT continue downstream tasks when any monitored kill criterion fails or cannot be evidenced.
- Do NOT weaken conflict rules, broaden the grammar, or cherry-pick the hold-out to manufacture a pass.
- Do NOT commit the private fixture, descriptions, normalized tokens, amounts, expected rows, or source paths.
- Do NOT use a second evaluator, external service, cloud model, or remote telemetry.
