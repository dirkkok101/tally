## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- Success, expected failure, handled unexpected failure, interruption, resume, abandon, cleanup, and startup recovery preserve 0700 directories, 0600 files, owner identity, and zero raw corpus copies or orphan temporaries.
- Canaries in descriptions, tokens, rules, corpus rows, amounts, requests, and failures appear in none of arguments, tracked files, stdout errors, stderr, logs, telemetry, crash output, reports, or unrelated results.
- Malformed JSON, unknown fields, symlinks, permissive modes, wrong owners, unknown files, outside-root paths, over-limits, and incompatible versions fail before activation or Ledger mutation.
- Every acceptance workflow completes with network denied, no prompt or TTY read, and no Tally daemon, watcher, scheduler, child process, or background worker before or after invocation.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT redact only logs while leaving payloads in arguments, errors, crash files, telemetry, tracked artifacts, or temporary storage.
- Do NOT skip permission, symlink, path traversal, crash, or network-denied cases on linux-x64.
- Do NOT use cloud inference, remote embeddings, third-party processing, a daemon, or a hidden child process.
- Do NOT mark this validation-only gate green from unit tests alone; execute the published Native-AOT binary.
