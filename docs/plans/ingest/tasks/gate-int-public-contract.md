## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- IngestExtensions explicitly registers exactly PdfTextLayoutAStatementAdapter, PdfTextLayoutBStatementAdapter, StatementAdapterRegistry, IngestDatabase/migrator/protection, LedgerContractClient, every handler/store, and six operation modules; no reflection scan or plugin loading occurs.
- OperationRegistry contains exactly 8 unique ingest.* IDs/paths and preserves exactly 38 unique ledger.* IDs/paths; at this milestone the combined total is 46. Ledger inventory assertions are changed from global-total to ledger-prefix exactness, so no Ledger operation/schema/error/example is removed or weakened.
- Every ingest descriptor has a concrete source-generated request/result type, complete stable errors/exits, limits, versions, help/example, mutation classification, and matching handler. The registry remains the sole source for dispatch, help, schema, compatibility, and snapshots.
- tally schema list/show reports the exact eight operations and two qualified variants without reading a source or opening ingest.db; unknown operation/field/malformed input/incompatible version fails before processing.
- Published success and expected-failure invocations emit exactly one stdout business envelope, metadata-only stderr, and documented exit categories; sourcePath is accepted only inside --input - or --input @file JSON.
- Release linux-x64 self-contained Native-AOT publish succeeds offline with no trim/dynamic-code warning, network listener, daemon, child process, or missing JSON metadata.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT auto-discover services/modules, hand-maintain a second schema manifest, add aliases/generic CRUD/import/run operations, or introduce HTTP — rejected per governing decisions.
- Do NOT change the meaning or count of ledger.* operations to add INGEST; preserve 38 Ledger descriptors and scope its inventory test by prefix.
- Do NOT globally register incomplete handlers before every producer task is complete.
- Do NOT expose private store paths, SQL, PdfPig types, source content, exceptions, or financial payloads in help/errors/diagnostics.
