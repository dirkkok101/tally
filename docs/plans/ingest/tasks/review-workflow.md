## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- ingest.inspect requires exact batchId plus manifestRevisionId and returns the complete owner-only manifest view: identities, versions, selected account, ordered record outcomes, immutable candidates, exclusions, duplicates, conflicts, controls, validations, and approval state.
- Inspect performs no mutation and deterministic repeated reads return semantically identical ordered content.
- Approve rehashes the stored canonical manifest, checks exact digest/revision, committable state, every record/control, active selected account, compatible adapter/manifest/Ledger versions, and trusted actor attribution before one atomic approval insert.
- Any blocked record, overlap, failed control, incompatible version, missing fact, digest mismatch, stale account, or absent actor returns a stable failure and leaves the revision unapproved.
- Requests cannot update amount, currency, date, originalDescription, sign, provenance, account, adapter, duplicate disposition, or version; changed meaning requires a new preview/revision and prior approval is inactive for commit.
- ReviewOperationModule produces inspect/approve descriptor bindings but global registration waits for GATE-INT-PUBLIC-CONTRACT.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT implement a generic save/update or inline manifest editor — rejected by DD-INGEST-CLI-OPERATION-CONTRACT and DD-INGEST-MANIFEST-IDENTITY-OVERLAP.
- Do NOT approve by batchId alone, mutable latest revision, or unchecked digest.
- Do NOT mutate Ledger or reread/reparse the source during inspect or approve.
- Do NOT log or send the sensitive manifest to stderr/telemetry; it is allowed only in explicit stdout business output.
