## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- BoundRevision requires plan detail, BudgetPosition, calculation schema, complete unique ordinals, known stable category identities, matching contract versions, shared snapshot and generation evidence, and exact member-to-position bucket and total reconciliation.
- NoBudgetPlan and NoActiveBudgetPlanRevision require plan detail, BudgetPosition, and calculation schema to be absent, preserve the complete ordered member set, and reconcile its checked sum exactly to ledgerBudgetActualTotal.
- Unknown or duplicate identities, missing ordinals, stale generation, incompatible version, invalid category lifecycle, overflow, mismatched fingerprint, or reconciliation failure returns one whole-report typed failure and no BoundInsightEvidence.
- CLASSIFY coverage gaps preserve Uncategorized membership and never cause a direct CLASSIFY call or inferred category.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT query a producer, mutate upstream state, or persist members inside CoherentEvidenceBinder — per DD-INSIGHTS-DETERMINISTIC-REPORT-COMPILER.
- Do NOT invoke BudgetPositionCalculator, re-bucket a returned BoundRevision position, fabricate a zero plan, or accept incomplete pagination — per DD-INSIGHTS-COHERENT-PUBLIC-EVIDENCE.
- Do NOT downgrade coherence, compatibility, integrity, completeness, or exact-arithmetic failures into signal-local unavailable states.
