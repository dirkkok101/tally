## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- InsightsOperationModule contains exactly insights.report.generate, insights.report.explain, insights.report.get, insights.report.list, insights.report.retain, insights.report.restate, insights.report.delete, insights.status.get, insights.backup.create, insights.backup.verify, and insights.restore.activate in canonical verb order; Discover remains shared system.schema.list/show.
- Every descriptor publishes deterministic request/result fingerprints, compatibility range, authority, mutability, idempotency requirement, released limits, stable errors, and exit categories without reading a store or producer.
- Retain, Restate, Delete, Backup, and Restore require actor attribution and idempotency; Generate, Explain, Get, List, Status, and Verify are read-only; unsupported versions and unknown fields fail before financial reads.
- Complex input is accepted only through stdin or an approved owner-only file boundary, exactly one business envelope uses stdout, and diagnostics are metadata-only.
- The released policy catalogue marks Forecast, Trend, Anomaly, and Attention as Deferred and exposes no executable formula, threshold, severity, or mutation operation for them.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT replace the eleven operations with a generic action-discriminator facade or arbitrary insights.execute — rejected per DD-INSIGHTS-CLI-OPERATION-CONTRACT.
- Do NOT add HTTP or FastEndpoints routes — rejected per DD-INSIGHTS-CLI-OPERATION-CONTRACT.
- Do NOT add repository, store, compiler, calculator, clock, mapper, mediator, event, or transport interfaces — rejected per DD-INSIGHTS-APPLICATION-ARCHITECTURE.
- Do NOT use reflection scanning, runtime-generated schemas, interactive prompts, financial process arguments, or serialized request/response logging.
- Do NOT create Save, Edit, Refresh, Alert, Notify, policy CRUD, permission, role, menu, translation, or frontend-key contracts.
