## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- The contract assembly exposes closed enums and immutable records for policy descriptors, plan states, exact money and rationals, windows, metrics, pace states, evidence states, explanations, provenance, and InsightReport.
- Canonical report contracts exclude raw merchant descriptions, canonical transaction payloads, producer cursors, private evaluation rows, and dated actual members; only BoundInsightEvidence may carry dated members as ephemeral compiler input.
- All money is signed checked Int64 ZAR minor units, rationals have a positive denominator and canonical normalization, IDs are opaque stable values, and every collection documents deterministic ordering.
- The semantic content fingerprint includes every visible metric, state, explanation, provenance item, policy version, and source identity while excluding derived reportId and contentFingerprint fields.
- Source-generated serialization rejects unknown fields and produces byte-stable schema fingerprints under repeated builds.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT use string constants for system-owned states or statuses; use closed enums per ADR-CORE-0004.
- Do NOT add HTTP DTOs, frontend models, EF entities, policy scripts, arbitrary formulas, or caller-authored report payloads.
- Do NOT place raw merchant text, canonical transactions, producer cursors, or private evaluation rows in any analytical contract.
- Do NOT include reportId or contentFingerprint in the semantic payload that is hashed.

### Expected Outputs

- Native-AOT-safe analytical contract models
- Analytical schema and privacy tests

### Constraints

- Use file-scoped namespaces, nullable annotations, immutable records, and no reflection-based serialization.

### Notes

- Lifecycle, recovery, idempotency, and private-receipt records belong to TASK-INSIGHTS-LIFECYCLE-CONTRACT-MODELS.
