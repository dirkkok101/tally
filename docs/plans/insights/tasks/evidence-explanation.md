## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- Every requested active or deferred catalogue kind appears exactly once in catalogue order as Supported, Unavailable, InsufficientEvidence, NotStarted, or Deferred with a stable typed reason.
- Signal-local unavailability preserves supported exact metrics and omits zero, baseline, confidence, and severity unless zero is an exact observed value.
- Coherence, compatibility, integrity, incomplete evidence, and exact-arithmetic failures remain whole-report failures and cannot be rendered as a report state.
- Supported explanations cite exact value, window, identity, evidence references, arithmetic rule, version, and limitations; unavailable explanations cite evaluated evidence, unmet rule, and permitted next action without inventing a result.
- Identical structured state and template version yield semantically identical channel-neutral text with no persuasion, recommendation, translation, notification, delivery wording, or raw merchant expansion.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT silently omit unsupported kinds, invent confidence or severity, or collapse whole-report failures into local states.
- Do NOT make explanations caller-authored, localized, persuasive, advisory, notification-oriented, or dependent on display-text ordering.
- Do NOT expose one public service per metric or signal â€” rejected per DD-INSIGHTS-DETERMINISTIC-REPORT-COMPILER.
