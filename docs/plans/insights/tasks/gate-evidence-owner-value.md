## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- An owner-only manifest supplied through TALLY_INSIGHTS_PRIVATE_EVALUATION_MANIFEST contains every required evidence shape, time-separated cases, disclosure canaries, one named owner decision, a raw BudgetPosition comparison, and explicit owner judgments for report usefulness, explanation sufficiency, and retention privacy cost.
- The real Generate path and InsightPolicyValidationHarness account for every case and emit a privacy-safe receipt with zero false high-severity outcomes and all canaries contained.
- The owner accepts at least one active exact-metric plus Linear-Pace report shape as materially improving the named decision over raw BudgetPosition inspection, accepts deterministic explanations for v1, and accepts optional immutable retention as worth its privacy cost.
- The tracked verification report contains only counts, fingerprints, threshold outcomes, policy/schema/tool versions, environment identity, timestamp, and owner acceptance booleans; no private path or financial payload appears.
- A failed or unevidenced usefulness, explanation, retention, privacy, or canary criterion exits nonzero and blocks every task in SP-INSIGHTS-02-RETAINED-HISTORY and SP-INSIGHTS-03-RECOVERY; the do-less path is reported rather than weakened.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT continue to retained-state implementation when the monitored owner-value criterion fails or remains unevidenced.
- Do NOT cherry-pick cases, weaken canaries, infer owner acceptance, fabricate representative data, or replace the real compiler with a second evaluator.
- Do NOT commit the private manifest, source names, paths, values, merchant text, expected rows, report bodies, or raw BudgetPosition comparison.
- Do NOT activate deferred Forecast, Trend, Anomaly, or Attention Item scope to manufacture value.
