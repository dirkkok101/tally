## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- Retain requires normalized analysis input, trusted P1 actor, non-blank reason, and idempotency identity and accepts no report payload, reportId, snapshotId, contentFingerprint, or provenance authored by the caller.
- Exact replay resolves before TimeProvider and producer access; a new request invokes the existing Generate path, allocates one opaque ULID snapshotId, and appends snapshot metadata, canonical report payload, fingerprint, actor, reason, and Succeeded outcome in one BEGIN IMMEDIATE transaction.
- The retained identity records analysis-request fingerprint, source and policy versions, report schema, content fingerprint, trusted creation time, actor, and reason; later producer or policy change cannot alter it.
- Changed-input replay conflicts; validation, generation, cancellation, SQLite, disk, or commit failure leaves no partial snapshot, payload, or successful replay outcome.
- Logs and errors contain only safe stable IDs, fingerprints, states, and counts.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT retain ordinary Generate results automatically, accept caller-authored report content, update an existing report, or store upstream member rows — rejected per DD-INSIGHTS-RETENTION-RESTATEMENT-LIFECYCLE.
- Do NOT split snapshot, payload, and replay writes across transactions or allocate trusted time before exact replay is checked.
- Do NOT add automatic expiry, cleanup, background work, multiple writers, or payload-bearing diagnostics.

### Expected Outputs

- RetainInsightReportCommand
- Atomic retained-generation tests

### Constraints

- Reuse GenerateInsightReportQuery and InsightsIdempotencyExecutor; duplicate analytical pipelines are forbidden.
