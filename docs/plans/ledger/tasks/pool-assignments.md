## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- Every recorded transaction exposes one atomically initialized unassigned pool projection; assign/correct transitions it to one active pool or back to explicit unassigned with actor, reason, time, and predecessor.
- Missing/inactive transaction, absent/archived pool, multiple-pool request, stale state, same-as-active correction, and conflicting replay fail without changing current assignment.
- Pool changes do not mutate transaction facts, account, category, payment attribution, evidence, or reconciliation history.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT support split pools unless OQ-LEDGER-15 invalidates and the PRD/design are revised â€” per DD-LEDGER-DIMENSIONAL-ATTRIBUTION.
- Do NOT infer pool from any other dimension or overwrite assignment history.
- Do NOT silently inherit pool across transaction supersession â€” rejected per DD-LEDGER-IMMUTABLE-HISTORY.

### Expected Outputs

- Pool assignment contracts, policy, store, handlers, module, and exact history tests
