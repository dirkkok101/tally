## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- match_existing revalidates evidence fingerprint, policy/version, current candidate uniqueness, conflicts, and exact basis inside one BEGIN IMMEDIATE transaction before appending decision and confirming link.
- create_statement_only requires a typed canonical fact whose account, amount, currency, and dates equal the evidence observation and atomically creates fact, unknown attribution, unassigned pool, decision, confirming link, and replay result.
- record_ambiguous preserves the complete candidate set and applies no confirming link; record_exception preserves code/reason; stale, unsupported, conflicting, or changed replay preserves prior state.
- Crash injection at every write boundary leaves the full outcome absent or committed; identical logical evidence and decision replay returns the existing outcome and never creates another transaction.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT trust the advisory projection token, guess among candidates, or create statement-only while a guard candidate exists — per DD-LEDGER-RECONCILIATION-CONTRACT.
- Do NOT implement Ledger-owned statement batches, manifests, previews, approvals, or receipts; INGEST owns them.
- Do NOT use transport message IDs as authority or commit idempotency after the finance effect — rejected per DD-LEDGER-IDEMPOTENT-MUTATIONS.
- Do NOT overwrite transaction, evidence, or decision history.

### Expected Outputs

- Reconciliation apply contracts/policy/store/handler/module and crash-atomic integration tests
