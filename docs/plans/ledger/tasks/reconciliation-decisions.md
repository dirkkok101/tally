## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- decision.get returns ordered history, prior and active transaction IDs, confirmed_existing or corrected_from_statement detail, deterministic-policy or owner authority, statement authority basis, actor, reason, policy/version, links, carry-forward IDs, relationship replacement IDs, and current state.
- confirm/reject require typed owner actor and reason; revoke/replace require the expected active predecessor and atomically retire/create links and decisions.
- Missing/inactive/incompatible candidate, evidence confirmed elsewhere, stale predecessor, conflicting replay, or write failure preserves prior active state with a stable result.
- Correction never deletes history, edits a fact, or permits one statement Evidence Record to confirm multiple active transactions.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT treat Hermes clarification as implicit owner intent; require typed owner assertion fields.
- Do NOT overwrite/delete decisions or links, silently inherit across ordinary supersession, or repair duplicates by editing facts â€” per DD-LEDGER-IMMUTABLE-HISTORY.
- Do NOT bypass logical decision idempotency or erase statement authority/carry-forward references.
