## Recipe

### Acceptance Checks

- AppendAsync records exactly one replacement fact and supersession, decision and confirming link, category and pool carry-forward, payment carry-forward or unknown state, and approved relationship replacement.
- Every row references the prior and active transaction IDs and governing decision; all original facts, evidence, dimensions, and relationships remain queryable.
- Any collaborator rejection or injected failure leaves all correction-effect tables unchanged; identical logical replay returns the existing effect.

### Failure Criteria

- Do NOT open or commit another transaction; LedgerMutationExecutor owns atomicity.
- Do NOT duplicate producer stores, overwrite history, move links or dimensions in place, or drop invalid relationships — per DD-LEDGER-IMMUTABLE-HISTORY.
- Do NOT expose INGEST batches, provider payloads, or transport identifiers — per DD-LEDGER-RECONCILIATION-CONTRACT.
